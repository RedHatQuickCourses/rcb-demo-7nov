Hands-on Lab

A Hands-on Lab provides practical experience to solidify your understanding of Ansible. In this session, you will actively engage with Ansible by performing essential setup and command execution.

  • Set up an Ansible Control Node and configure Managed Hosts, establishing the environment for automation.

  • Execute Ad-hoc Commands on Remote Hosts, demonstrating immediate, single-task operations across your infrastructure.

This practical approach ensures you gain direct experience with core Ansible functionalities.
